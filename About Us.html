Youth Leadership and Advocacy.

Who are we?

Youth Leadership and Advocacy is a youth-led organization registered with the government of Sierra Leone through the National Youth Commission to complement the effort of government in youth development and gender empowerment to mitigate youth unemployment and gender-based violence in Sierra Leone.

Our Mandate "What We Do"

  1. Organize youth empowerment programs/conferences.
  2. Organize Capacity Building Training/Seminars for youth groups.
  3. Advocate for gender empowerment, inclusion & participation,

Our Mission.

To create a platform where young people realize their fullest potential for national development.

Our Vision

A reformed society that allows youths to enhance their enormous potentials.

Our Values and Principles

  1. Equality and Equity
  2. Independence
  3. Professionalism
  4. Accountability and Transparency
  5. Integrity
