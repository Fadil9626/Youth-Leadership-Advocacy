Online Introduction of Youth Leadership and Advocacy (YLA)

Meet the Deputy Public Relations Officer of Youth Leadership & Advocacy, Miss Agnes Barber. Agnes Barber was born on the 22nd of March. She attended the Annie Walsh Memorial School where she got her high school certificate, and she’s presently a final year student, pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Mass Communications at the Fourah Bay College, University of Sierra Leone. Agnes was a radio presenter at Kids Radio (2010-2014) where she used her voice to promote the voice of children and young people, during this period she provided a platform where young people discuss issues relating to their development and promote national cohesion. As an intern at the first technology company in Sierra Leone, known as Afro Technology and Logistics (ATL) she added value to the mission and vision of the company, her human relationships paved her way to rise through the ranks of leadership at the company and now she serving as Executive Assistant to the Chief Executive Officer. Also, she’s presently working with Eminence Africa Entertainment, a branding, and marketing, live band, record label, event management, talent management, photography, and videography that introduces new arts to light up the pleasure in the entertainment industry. Agnes is an emerging entrepreneur, she founded Agie’s Frozen Yogurt, a business that generates money to take care of young girls in deprived communities. Because of her passion for youth development and mitigating the violence against women and girls she joined Youth Leadership and Advocacy and presently, she's serving in the capacity of Deputy Public Relations Officer.
