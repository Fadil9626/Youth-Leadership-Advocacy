Online Introduction of Youth Leadership and Advocacy (YLA)

Meet the Deputy Executive Director for Youth Leadership & Advocacy Ms. Mariama Yansaneh. Ms. Yansaneh was born and raised in Sierra Leone. She attended Forum for Africa Women Educationalist Primary School ( F. A. W. E). Did her Junior and Secondary School at the Government Model Secondary School where she was served as the Senior Prefect during her Junior School and also served as the Head Girl at the Senior Level. At Model Secondary School, she was the President of the Government Secondary School News Journal, where she aimed at ensuring she start practicing as young journalists. She was able to work with different schools across the country to promote students journalism. Ms. Yansaneh worked at the Premier Media Radio on a Radio Program called "Pikin Voice” and Culture Radio to advocate for children that are less privileged. Her aim was to talk on behalf of children who are suffering and faced with sexual gender based violence. Moreover, Ms. Yansaneh gained admission at Fourah Bay College, University of Sierra Leone. Presently she's a third-year student pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Social Work. At the University, She is a volunteer at Social Workers Sierra Leone, where they cater to support the less privileged people, people with disability, and those with mental issues, etc.
