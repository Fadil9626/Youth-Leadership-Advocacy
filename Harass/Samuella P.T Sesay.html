Online Introduction of Youth Leadership and Advocacy (YLA)

Samuella P.T Sesay attended Convent Secondary School, she has an outstanding performance in writing and publications, during her high school days she participated in numerous writing and Spelling Bee competitions. Samuella was part of the Literary Debating Society where she served as Secretary General for two consecutive years, she published high school journals and also co-chair a committee on article publications. Samuella is currently a third-year student at Fourah Bay College pursuing a BSc in Social Work. She benefited from the national girls camp initiative by Mrs. Nyama Koroma former First Lady of Sierra Leone. Miss Sesay is currently the Deputy Secretary-General for Youth Leadership and Advocacy.
