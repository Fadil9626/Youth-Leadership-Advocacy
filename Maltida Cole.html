Online Introduction of Youth Leadership and Advocacy (YLA)

Matilda Cole was born and raised in the west part of Freetown, she attended Covent Secondary School for Girls where she earned her high school diploma, and currently, she's pursuing her degree in Social Work from the Fourah Bay College University of Sierra Leone. Matilda Cole is known for her quality in organizing programs for both public and private sectors, she has a track record of brilliant ideas in organizing programs and championing services to put smiles on the faces of her customers. Base on her record in organizing high-standard programs, Cole is currently serving as a deputy organizer for Youth Leadership and Advocacy.
